SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A School Management System (SMS) is a software application designed to automate and streamline various administrative, academic, and financial activities within a school. It helps schools efficiently manage students, teachers, courses, fees, attendance, examinations, and other operations. With the advancement of technology, schools are moving towards digital transformation, and an effective SMS plays a crucial role in ensuring smooth school operations while improving communication between administrators, teachers, students, and parents. SMS Stores and manages student details like name, date of birth, admission number, and contact details. Keeps records of student attendance, and the academic performance, and disciplinary actions. Allows tracking of student progress and generating reports SMS Maintains records of all the teachers and staff, including personal details, qualifications, and pay roll. Helps in assigning subjects and classes to teachers. Tracks teachers’ attendance and performance SMS Automates the admission process by handling applications online. Manages student enrollment and all the document verification. Assigns students to classes and sections efficiently. SMS Records student and teacher attendance digitally. Generates automated reports for analysis. Sends real-time attendance updates to parents. SMS Helps in creating and managing school timetables. try Avoids scheduling conflicts between subjects and classrooms. Notifies teachers and students about changes in the timetable. SMS Conducts online and offline examinations. Generates and evaluates report cards automatically. Helps in analyzing student performance and progress.
